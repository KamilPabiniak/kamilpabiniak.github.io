  • B.A.R - May 2023

    Project B.A.R - Be Aware of Restrictions. In this game you play the role of a bartender who runs his establishment in the fictional city of Tatzerma. Every day you have to make difficult moral decisions that will affect the fate of your bar and customers. In addition, life is complicated by an authoritarian government that introduces constant changes in regulations and restrictions, making your job more difficult.
    The game is modeled on the climate of Papers Please.

    I was one of two programmers on the team which gave me a new perspective on how to work with someone. From the other programmer I learned programming principles to keep the code in order. While working, I learned how to work on several scenes so as to separate UI, minigames or loading screen. We had a timeframe of 3 months for the work, and I will be happy to tell what else I managed to accomplish in the project. The project ultimately did not receive investor support, and I myself cannot provide a build of the game.

  • Figa de Gattino - Game Jam - 2024

    At MageJam vol. 7 at Collegium da Vinci, we created a game in just 32 hours.
    As the only programmer among 3 graphic designers and a designer, it was a challenge. The tight deadline stressed us out but also gave us excitement, allowing us to focus on key aspects thanks to our previous experience. I demonstrated my ability to quickly solve problems such as collisions and enemy variety.

    I developed an interesting system for spawning enemies around the player, which smartly prevented enemies from appearing in areas blocking movement. At the demonstration, I was proud of the condition in which we left the project.
